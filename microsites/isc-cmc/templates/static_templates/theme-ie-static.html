Coordinate My Care is a clinical service that allows health and social care professionals to create urgent care plans reflecting patients' clinical needs, wishes and preferences to be shared with the urgent care providers 24/7.

% if show_homepage_promo_video: View a sample video % endif

Online training from CMC is only available to users with modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, IE9 or later. You can download PDF versions of each module and view module videos using the links below. If you encounter problems viewing the online videos, they can be downloaded and watched offline. To do this: right click on the video link and choose "Save link as..." (Chrome) or "Save target as..." (IE). To play a video a compatible MP4 video player must be installed locally on your machine.

CMC108: Patient Lists

For clinical and administrative users. This module explains the functionality of the Patient Lists screen in the CMC system, which allows users to search for groups of patients whose care plans meet different criteria. From the Patient Lists screen, users can also create saved lists of patients or saved searches to run again in the future.

CMC109: Subscribing to CMC Care Plans

For clinical and administrative users. This module explains the functionality of CMC care plan subscriptions, which allow a clinical or administrative userto receive notifications about changes to a patientâs care plan. CMC users can proactively choose patients they would like to track and select one or more subscription events—significant changes to a care plan—to follow. Users receive an email notification when those events occur, and therefore are aware if changes occur that may affect care.
