FAQ

How do I sign up for a course?

Simply create an account and then register for the course of your choice. Choose Register Now to get started.


What are the requirements for taking an online course on this system?

  • The courses work best with current versions of Chrome, Firefox or Safari, or with Internet Explorer version 10 or above.

  • Adobe Flash player 10.


Who do I contact if I experience a technical issue while taking an online course?

Email CMC_training@nhs.net.


Who do I contact if I have a question about the content of a course?

Email CMC_training@nhs.net.


What is the cost of taking an online course?

The initial online courses are offered free of charge. InterSystems Corporation reserves the right to change this policy in the future.


How do I suggest new topics?

Email your suggestions to: onlinetraining@intersystems.com.


Is there a way for me to be notified when new courses are added to the system?

You can sign up for our mailing list to receive updates about InterSystems Learning Services initiatives.


Can I download a copy of the Caché to use to follow along with the courses?

You can download a free single user license of Caché from the Software Downloads page on our website. Supported customers can also download pre-released versions of Ensemble and HealthShare from the same link using their WRC login.

Copies of Ensemble and HealthShare are not available in a free trial version.
